Generating amino acid sequence for protein synthesis

Collaborative research project (Prof. Raschka and Prof. Romero)

The goal of the project is to synthesize protein from a certain family using deep learning models and could be potentially used to design protein with desired properties. To this end, I implement the Char-RNN and the transformer model using PyTorch.
